Game Systems

Leveling • Stats • Skills • Prestige

Leveling (1 → 200)

Progression is about transformation: attribute fragments, milestone unlocks, and prestige re-emergence. Leveling should feel impactful and permanent.

  • Boss drops can grant permanent attribute fragments.
  • Exploration and crafting grant small but meaningful growth.
  • Prestige at caps unlocks legendary paths per faction.

Stats & Stat Points

Stats come in Core, Hidden, and Faction layers to reinforce identity and playstyle.

  • Core: STR, DEX, INT, VIT, END
  • Hidden: Luck, Perception, Charisma, Resistances, Lifesteal, Mana Pool
  • Faction Bonuses: Radiance, Leadership, Corruption, Affinity

Skills & Skill Points

Skill lines level independently (Class / Weapon / Armor / Faction / Universal). Choices branch via morphs; late-game unlocks awakenings and legendary lines.

  • ESO-style Skill Lines & Morphs
  • BDO-style Awakening vs Succession
  • Legendary quests unlock prestige-only abilities

Prestige

Re-emerge to advanced identities, keep your legacy, and pursue faction-themed legendary quests.

  • Permanent passives and cosmetic auras
  • Faction-tuned capstone quests
  • Account-wide legacy perks (mount mastery, storage)
