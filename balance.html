Factions Systems Balance

Balance Philosophy

Light vs Shadow • Order vs Chaos — circular parity, no hard winners.

Holy Empire ⚔ Shadowborn

Radiance and sustain counter poison and corruption; ambush and DoTs pressure healing windows.

Knights Order ⚔ Arcane Order

Formations, nullifiers, and anti-magic discipline meet burst damage, AoE control, and chaos manipulation.

Design Tenets

  • No faction dominates outright — advantages are situational.
  • Counterplay is readable (shields vs DoTs, nullifiers vs bursts).
  • Group synergy matters: buffs, formations, and roles.
